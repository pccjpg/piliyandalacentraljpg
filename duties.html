Core Pillars of Service

Our functional identity revolves around the classic message embedded within our college crest: "Bodetha Pabodetha Dametha" (Awareness, Enlightenment, and Discipline).

As junior leaders, we turn these core values into real-world action through a structural system of daily duty tracks, special event safety controls, and community outreach operations across the school grounds.

Daily Operational Spheres

The practical execution of student governance is divided into three distinct operational time blocks every school day:

01. Morning Gates & Uniform Checks

Timeframe: 6:45 AM - 7:25 AM

Guild members stand at the primary school gates to monitor student arrival promptness. Prefects assist faculty in observing uniform compliance, proper badge placement, and overall student neatness before morning sessions begin.

02. Session Intervals & Ground Controls

Timeframe: 10:15 AM - 10:45 AM

During recreation breaks, assigned squads patrol classroom corridors, the school cafeteria area, and primary playgrounds to prevent reckless activities and support general student safety.

03. Assembly Management & Dismissal

Timeframe: Variations & 1:45 PM

Prefects align classes into orderly rows for morning assemblies and coordinate structured lines during school closing times, helping clear paths and preventing congestion at main exit points.

Special Event Protocol & Logistics

Beyond daily schedules, the Junior Prefects' Guild acts as a primary operations unit during major school functions, executing specific coordination checklists:

  • Inter-House Sports Meets
    Managing spectator house sectors, escorting special guests, tracking official timing desks, and securing perimeter lines around athletic tracks.
  • Religious & Cultural Events (e.g., Poson Bathi Gee)
    Setting up light exhibits, organizing general visitor sitting layouts, guiding parent crowds, and working with security panels to handle incoming traffic blocks.
  • Annual Prize Givings & Assemblies
    Arranging internal main stage decorations, keeping strict award sequence charts aligned, and guiding guests to assigned seating tiers.

The Prefect's Code of Conduct

"To lead by example before directing others. Authority granted to a prefect is measured strictly by their capacity to serve with humility and absolute honesty."

Impartiality

Applying rule matrices fairly to all peers without personal bias or favor.

Punctuality

Arriving ahead of general school alerts and completing logs reliably.

Integrity

Maintaining elite classroom attention levels and exemplary character reviews.
